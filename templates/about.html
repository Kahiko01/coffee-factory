About KCFMS

Kenya Coffee Factory Management System is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline coffee production management from farm to cup. We empower farmers, cooperatives, and administrators with powerful tools for tracking, payments, and analytics.

Key Features

🌱 Farmer Management

Complete farmer registration and profile management system with role-based access control.

📊 Yield Tracking

Real-time tracking of daily, weekly, monthly, and seasonal coffee production yields.

💰 Payment Automation

Automated payment calculations based on quantity and quality grades with PDF statements.

📈 Reports & Analytics

Comprehensive reporting system with export capabilities to PDF and Excel formats.

🔔 Smart Notifications

Automated SMS and in-app notifications for deliveries, payments, and announcements.

🎯 Quality Grading

Integrated quality grading system (Premium, A, B, C) with dynamic pricing.
